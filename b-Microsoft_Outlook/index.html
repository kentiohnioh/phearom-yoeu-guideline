Microsoft Outlook Login Guide

Follow these simple steps to access your Outlook account
By Phearom Yoeu
1
Navigate to Microsoft Sign-in Page

Begin by opening your preferred web browser and navigating to the Microsoft sign-in page. You can typically find this by going to outlook.live.comClicking this will take you to Outlook or office.comOffice 365 login portal.

  • Look for the Sign in button in the top-right corner
  • You'll be redirected to the Microsoft authentication page
  • The URL should begin with login.microsoftonline.com
Note

Make sure you're on the official Microsoft website before entering your credentials to avoid phishing attempts.

2
Enter Your Email Address

On the Microsoft sign-in page, you'll see a field asking for your email, phone, or Skype.

  • Enter your full Microsoft account email address (e.g., yourname@gmaill.com)
  • Double-check for any typos in your email address
  • Click Next to proceed
Important

If you don't have an account yet, click "Create one" to register for a new Microsoft account before proceeding.

3
Enter Your Password

After entering your email, you'll be prompted to enter your password.

  • Type your account password carefully (passwords are case-sensitive)
  • If you've forgotten your password, click Forgot password? to reset it
  • For security, the password will appear as dots as you type
  • Click Sign in when ready
Security Tip

If you're using a public or shared computer, consider using "Private Browsing" mode and never check "Keep me signed in".

4
Stay Signed In? (Optional)

Microsoft may ask if you want to stay signed in to your account.

  • Select Yes if this is your personal device and you want to skip signing in next time
  • Select No if this is a public or shared computer for better security
  • This preference can be changed later in your account settings
Security Recommendation

We recommend choosing "No" unless you're on a trusted personal device. Even then, consider using app passwords for added security.

5
Access Your Outlook Inbox

Congratulations! You've successfully signed in to your Outlook account.

  • Your inbox will display all received emails
  • Use the left sidebar to navigate between Inbox, Sent Items, Drafts, and other folders
  • Click on any email to view its contents
Customization Tip

You can customize your Outlook interface by dragging folders to rearrange them or right-clicking to create new folders for better organization.
